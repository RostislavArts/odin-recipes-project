Fresh Tomato Soup

Recipe was taken from AllRecipes

These baked feta eggs are so very simple to make, but a great way to style up breakfast, brunch, or dinner.

Delicious with garlic bread or a grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients:

  1. Tomatoes
  2. Chicken broth
  3. Garlic
  4. Onion
  5. Butter
  6. Flour
  7. Sugar
  8. Salt

Directions:

Gather the ingredients.

Boil the tomatoes, onion, garlic, and broth.

Run the mixture through a food mill into a large bowl.

Make a roux.

Add the tomato mixture and season.
